# 🏙️ Citizen Complaint Management System

## 📌 Project Overview

The Citizen Complaint Management System is a smart civic issue reporting platform designed to improve communication between citizens and municipal authorities. It enables citizens to report real-time civic problems such as potholes, water leakage, electricity failures, sanitation issues, and other public infrastructure concerns using GPS-tagged images and videos.

The platform enhances transparency, accountability, and service efficiency through real-time complaint tracking, AI-powered analysis, automated department routing, and comprehensive administrative dashboards.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Urban local bodies and municipal corporations often struggle to identify, prioritize, and resolve civic issues efficiently due to:

* Manual and paper-based complaint systems
* Lack of real-time complaint tracking
* Missing visual evidence and accurate location data
* Poor inter-departmental coordination
* Delayed responses and unresolved complaints
* Limited transparency and accountability

These challenges reduce citizen trust and hinder effective public service delivery.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

The system provides a centralized digital platform where:

* Citizens submit complaints using live image/video capture with automatic GPS tagging
* Complaints are automatically categorized and routed to the correct department
* AI-powered analysis assists in severity detection and duplicate complaint identification
* Real-time status tracking improves transparency
* Staff and administrators manage complaints through dashboards, analytics, and reporting tools
